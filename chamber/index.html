SUPPORT LOCAL GEMS

Idaho’s small businesses are the backbone of our state’s economy, but for almost a year, the pandemic has posed a significant challenge to these vital enterprises. Now more than ever, it’s important to support the small businesses that make the Gem State special. The Support Local Gems: All Season Long initiative is a holiday revival of the statewide Support Local Gems public awareness campaign encouraging Idahoans to Support Local Gems – the small businesses in our communities – that have been impacted by the pandemic.
